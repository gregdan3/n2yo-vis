Space the final frontier... Wait..

This project graphs satelites in space against the date that they were launched. You have the ability to graph based on the satellite's apogee or perigee.

- Use the slider at the top of the page to check out different years.
- Switch between apogee and perigee by clicking the botton in the top right
- Hold shift+scroll to zoom in on different parts of the graph.
